My Projects.

During my time at university and in my work placements I have had the chance to take on many projects. I have had a lot of fun on these projects and displaying them here allows me to show what I have done.

Dissertation - Creating an Enigma Machine.

In my final year of University, I am required to do a dissertation project. I chose to develop an Enigma machine and compare it to modern day encryption methods to compare and underpin key advancements in industry. This will be done to recommend different ways a business can keep their data secure and help protect against cryptographic attacks such as WannaCry. Development will start November 2019 and will be coded in JavaScript.

Fujitsu - Creating a Risk Management Tool.

While at Fujistu I was tasked with creating a Tool to be a response to the demise of IS2. This tool took in Risks, Threats, Vulnerabilities and Controls and compared them all to generate a bunch of risk metalanguage statements and rate the risk from very high to low. This tool was not meant to replace a risk assessment but to assist in a full risk assessment. This tool was entirely coded in C# as this was the language, I was studying at University at the time in hindsight this probably would have been better as a web application especially with the push to the cloud in recent years.

Agile Development - Creating a Timetable System.

While at University in my second year I took a Module based on Agile software development to start thinking in an Agile way. This was leaps and bounds over what was already in place and we were planning to present it to the University. However, the university had already chosen to implement a new system when we finished development.
